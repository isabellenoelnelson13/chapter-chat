Privacy Policy

Chapter Chat  ·  Effective date: June 7, 2025  ·  Last updated: June 7, 2025

Chapter Chat ("we," "our," or "us") is a reading tracker and social app for book lovers. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account & Profile

  • Email address — collected when you create an account with email and password, or via Sign in with Apple.
  • Display name and username — chosen by you during sign-up.
  • Bio and profile photo — optional, set in your profile settings.

Reading Activity

  • Books on your shelves — titles you add to Reading, Want to Read, Read, or Did Not Finish.
  • Reading sessions — start page, end page, duration, and date for each session you log.
  • Progress and ratings — current page, completion percentage, and star ratings you assign to books.
  • Reviews and notes — written reviews you compose in the app.

Social & Community Content

  • Activity feed posts — events such as starting or finishing a book that you choose to share to your feed.
  • Comments and likes — interactions you make on other users' activity.
  • Direct messages — messages you send and receive through the in-app messaging feature.
  • Book club activity — posts, suggestions, and votes within clubs you join or create.
  • Follow relationships — which accounts you follow and who follows you.

Device & Technical

  • Push notification token — generated by your device to deliver notifications you opt in to.
  • Search queries — book titles or author names you search for within the app.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, browsing history, financial information, or health data.

PurposeData used

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Data Storage

Your account data, library, sessions, messages, and social content are stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL database and object storage), hosted on AWS infrastructure. Profile photos are stored in Supabase Storage. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

ServicePurposeData shared
Supabase Database, authentication, file storage, real-time messaging All user data described above
Anthropic (Claude) AI book recommendations and reading list curation Your book titles, authors, ratings, and shelf status (no personal identifiers)
Hardcover Book search catalog Search queries (no personal identifiers)
Google Books API Book cover image search Search queries (no personal identifiers)
Expo Push Service Delivering push notifications Push notification token and notification payload

All AI requests (Anthropic) and catalog requests (Hardcover, Google Books) are routed through our server-side functions. Your API keys and personal identifiers are never exposed to these third parties directly.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data — including your profile, library, reading sessions, reviews, messages, and social activity — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

To delete your account, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email us at isabellenoelnelson@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at isabellenoelnelson@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Chapter Chat is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we'll notify you through the app or by email.
